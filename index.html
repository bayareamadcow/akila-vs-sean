How I Judged Value

评价标准 / Value Criteria

1. 能不能先创造优势

谁可以在半场进攻里先迫使防守改变位置。

Who can create the first advantage and force the defense to move.

2. 能不能让队友更好打

不是只看自己得分，也看传球、牵制、节奏、空间感。

Not just personal scoring, but passing, gravity, tempo, and spacing feel.

3. 可替代性

这个角色如果少了，球队有没有别人能比较自然地补上。

If this player is missing, how easily can the team replace his role?

4. 阵容适配度

和不同队友一起上时，价值会不会掉很多。

Whether his value travels across different lineups.

Most Valuable Overall

Yang Zhou #23

Primary pressure player / 第一层进攻压力点

优点 / Strengths

Yang 最大价值是能自己先把防守打乱。他的身体、直线启动和推进感会让对面收缩， 这会给外线、切入和二次处理制造空间。

Yang gives the team the strongest first pressure. His strength, straight-line drive, and willingness to push downhill can collapse the defense and open the next pass or cut.

需要改进 / Weaknesses

有些回合如果第一下没过去，容易继续硬打。对球队来说，下一步最好是更早读协防， 多把球传到第二侧，让自己的突破威胁变成全队优势。

When the first move is stopped, he can sometimes keep forcing the same lane. The next jump is earlier help reads and quicker second-side passes.

最适合的用法 / Best Role

开局和关键回合应该让他先碰球，尤其需要破僵局时。他不一定每回合都要终结， 但应该负责先让防守移动。

Let him touch the ball early in possessions and in stuck moments. He does not need to finish every action, but he should often be the player who makes the defense move first.

队内价值 / Team Value

排第一，因为他的角色最难替代。没有这个第一层压力，Akila 和 Sean 的很多优势都会变得更难发挥。

He ranks first because his role is the hardest to replace. Without first-layer pressure, the cleaner touches for Akila and Sean become harder to create.

Closest To First

Akila #88 / #69

Connector and lineup stabilizer / 连接器和阵容稳定器

优点 / Strengths

Akila 的价值不是只在一两个漂亮回合里，而是在很多普通回合里让球不死。 他能接住第二拍，做下一次传导或处理，让队友继续在节奏里打。

Akila's value shows up in normal possessions. He keeps the ball alive, handles the second beat, and helps teammates keep playing in rhythm.

需要改进 / Weaknesses

他有时可以更主动一点。如果防守已经把他当连接点放松，他需要用更果断的出手或突破惩罚对面。

He can be more assertive. If defenses treat him as only a connector, he should punish that with quicker shots or decisive drives.

最适合的用法 / Best Role

最适合和 Yang 一起上。Yang 先压防守，Akila 接第二侧处理，这样球队会更顺。 关键时刻他适合留在场上，因为他不太破坏阵容结构。

He fits best next to Yang. Yang bends the defense first, then Akila organizes the second side. He is a strong closing option because he keeps lineup structure intact.

队内价值 / Team Value

排第二，但和第一差距不大。如果队里已经有足够第一创造，Akila 的价值可能会接近甚至超过别人， 因为他最能提升整体流畅度。

He ranks second, close to first. If the team already has enough first creation on the floor, his value can become extremely high because he improves the whole possession.

Best As Advantage Finisher

Sean #1

Catch-and-go scorer / 接球顺打得分点

优点 / Strengths

Sean 最好的价值是在球已经转起来以后接球攻击。他有节奏、有速度， 如果接球点干净，可以很快把一个小优势变成上篮、罚球线附近机会或继续压缩防守。

Sean is most valuable after the ball has already moved. With a clean catch, he can turn a small advantage into a drive, paint touch, or another defensive rotation.

需要改进 / Weaknesses

如果让他从静态单打开始，价值会下降。下一步要提高无球选位和接球前准备， 让自己更常拿到已经有优势的球。

His value drops when he has to start from a static isolation. The next step is better off-ball positioning and being ready before the catch.

最适合的用法 / Best Role

不需要让他一直当第一发起点。更好的用法是弱侧接球、45 度接球、转换中提前跑位， 让他用第一步攻击已经移动的防守。

He does not need to be the constant first initiator. Use him on weak-side catches, 45-degree catches, and transition lanes so he attacks a defense that is already moving.

队内价值 / Team Value

排第三，不是因为不重要，而是因为他的最高价值比较依赖队友先创造优势。 如果球队能给他更舒服的接球点，他的实际贡献会明显上升。

He ranks third, not because he is unimportant, but because his best value depends more on teammates creating the first advantage. With cleaner catches, his impact rises fast.

Team Usage

怎么用最合理 / Practical Team Takeaways

Closing lineup

关键时刻优先 Yang + Akila 同场。第五人按需求选：要得分选 Sean/Eaxin，要身体选 Willy/James。

Prioritize Yang + Akila in closing groups. Pick the last spots by need: Sean/Eaxin for scoring, Willy/James for size.

When offense is stuck

先找 Yang，但提前设计 Akila 的第二接应点，避免 Yang 每次都被迫硬打。

Start with Yang, but pre-place Akila as the second outlet so Yang does not have to force every finish.

When bench units play

给 Chris/Tiger 简单任务：防守、跑位、快速传下一点。不要让他们承担太复杂的静态创造。

Give Chris/Tiger simple jobs: defend, space, run, and move the ball. Do not overload them with static creation.
