优点 / Strengths

His shooting gravity is real tactical value; drives and transition pressure collapse the defense quickly.

Primary attack point with a second option: Willy threes, James/Eaxin midrange, Akila crashes.

队内价值 / Team Value

最高核心价值之一。即使某场低分，他的 gravity 仍然会给队友创造空间。

One of the highest-value core pieces. Even in low-scoring games, his gravity creates space.

Willy #26

Starting shooter + floor spacer / 主力 shooter + 空间点

Corner and wing shooting open driving lanes; his paired gravity with Yang is the spacing core.

最适合的用法 / Best Use

每场设计 3-5 次明确三分机会。少运球，多 relocate，多接球即投。

Design 3-5 clear threes per game. Less dribbling, more relocation, more catch-and-shoot.

队内价值 / Team Value

首发级价值。追分阵容必上，因为他让防守不敢收缩。

Starter-level value and a must in comeback lineups because he prevents defensive collapse.

Starting Big / Screens / Rebounds

Screener + rebounder + midrange release / 掩护篮板和中投出口

优点 / Strengths

The frontcourt piece in the best lineup. Low scoring does not erase his screen/rebound/body value.
